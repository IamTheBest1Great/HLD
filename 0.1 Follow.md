## üß† The 6-Step Interview Framework (Practice It Every Time)

1. **Requirements (5 min)** ‚Äì Functional, non-functional, out of scope.
2. **Estimation (5 min)** ‚Äì Traffic, storage, bandwidth.
3. **API Design (5 min)** ‚Äì Endpoints, request/response.
4. **High-Level Architecture (10 min)** ‚Äì Draw components and data flow.
5. **Deep Dive (15 min)** ‚Äì Zoom into 1-2 critical components (database, cache, queue).
6. **Bottlenecks & Trade-offs (5 min)** ‚Äì Identify SPOFs, scaling limits, and justify choices.
